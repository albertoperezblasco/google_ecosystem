# Gemini Deep Research (Apps)

## TL;DR

The Gemini app research surface for end users that plans, browses, and compiles multi-page reports on their behalf. It fits when the user wants a consumer research experience rather than a developer agent surface.

## What It Is

Gemini Deep Research in the Gemini app is the end-user counterpart to the developer-side `Gemini Deep Research Agent`.

The consumer experience is built around selecting Deep Research in Gemini, letting it run a longer research job, and then reviewing the resulting report. It can use public web sources and, when available, the user's own Workspace context.

## When To Use It

- When the user wants Gemini to investigate a topic over many sources and return a fuller report.
- When a background research job is more appropriate than immediate chat.
- When the workflow belongs in the Gemini app rather than in a developer integration.

## When It Is Not The Best Fit

- If the need is a programmable research agent inside an application, use `Gemini Deep Research Agent`.
- If the task is source-grounded work over a user-curated notebook, use `NotebookLM`.
- If the need is only ordinary chat, use `Gemini Apps`.

## Key Capabilities

| Capability | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- |
| Background research runs | Lets the user hand off longer investigative tasks |
| Multi-page report output | Produces something more structured than a chat reply |
| Consumer-friendly experience | Keeps the workflow in the Gemini app instead of developer tooling |
